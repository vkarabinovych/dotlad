# Support

Dotlad is maintained primarily for the author's personal use and published
as-is. It does not provide individual setup, troubleshooting, compatibility,
or integration support.

## What to expect

- The repository documentation is the primary support resource.
- There is no public roadmap or response-time commitment.
- Only repository collaborators can open bug reports, feature requests, and
  pull requests.
- Releases follow the maintainer's own needs rather than a published schedule.
- Forks are welcome for workflows or priorities that differ from the project.

Before 1.0, minor releases may include breaking CLI, manifest, or resolver
changes. Pin a release tag or submodule revision and review the changelog before
updating.

## Security

Do not disclose suspected vulnerabilities publicly. Follow the private
reporting process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Security reports are also
handled on a best-effort basis without a guaranteed response or fix timeline.
